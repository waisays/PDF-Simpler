Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro plans?
The Free plan includes 20 tasks per day with all basic PDF tools. The Pro plan removes all limits with unlimited tasks, priority processing, advanced analytics, and additional features like batch processing and higher file size limits.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover through our secure Stripe payment system.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use Stripe for payment processing, which is PCI DSS compliant and uses industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.
What happens if I exceed my daily limit on the Free plan?
If you reach your 20-task daily limit on the Free plan, you'll need to wait until the next day or upgrade to Pro for unlimited tasks. The limit resets automatically at midnight.
