Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Simpler free to use? ▼
Yes! PDF Simpler offers a free tier with 20 tasks per day. No credit card required. For unlimited tasks and advanced features, upgrade to Pro for just $4.99/month.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. All files are processed securely and automatically deleted within 2 hours. We use industry-standard encryption and are GDPR compliant.
What file types are supported? ▼
We support PDF, Word (DOC, DOCX), Excel (XLS, XLSX), PowerPoint (PPT, PPTX), images (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP), HTML, and text files.
Do I need to install software? ▼
No installation required! PDF Simpler works entirely in your web browser. Just upload your file and process it instantly.
What's the file size limit? ▼
You can upload files up to 100MB. For larger files, consider our Pro plan which offers increased limits.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
